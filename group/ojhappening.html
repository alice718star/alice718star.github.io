What New Yorkers are doing!

Freebies

New York City has the reputation of being one of the most expensive cities in the World. From food to housing to clothing the cost of living is New York City is costly. While this is true, New York is also known to have an extensive list of free activities and amenities. New Yorkers can use their NY library card to get into 32 places for free including the MoMA and Guggenheim.

For Kids

What's NYC without the kids? Kids in NYC enjoy various amenities, events and activites throughout the year. Summer is one of the favorite seasons for NYC kids because they get to enjoy the outdoors. Raising 2 boys in NYC means being creative with time. Some of the activities we take advantage of include NYC playrounds, NYC Parks, NYC Water Parks, NYC street fairs and NYC amusement parks

Culture

NYC is one of the only places in the world you will meet people from various countries around the world. New Yorkers from African origin make up a large percent of the NYC population. Harlem is the central place to go to enjoy everything African. From food, to shopping, to hair braiding to street art. Harlem is also the home of the famous Apollo Theater and Magic Johnson Theater. Recently NYC's very own Cardi B. Her fame is credited for her being a true New Yorker.

Entertainment

There is no debate about NYC being the capital of Music and Entertainment. NYC created an extensive list of some of the world's most famous musicians and actors. A visit to the Madame Tussauds located in Times Square will give you access to some of the World's entertainers including our very own New Yorkers' wax figures.
