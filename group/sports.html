Sports in NYC

New York Yankees

The New York Yankees is the most well known professional American baseball team in the United States. Located in the heart of the Bronx, Yankee stadium is home for this team. With the capacity to hold 57,545 baseball fans, this stadium caters to those from all walks of life who wish to enjoy the game.

New York Knicks & Brooklyn Nets

The New York Knicks and Brooklyn Nets are New York City's home basketball teams. Their home arenas are respectively located in Madison Square Garden and the Barclay's Center. With no end to excitement, each teams plays 41 games each year while famous Director Spike Lee has a constant presence at Knicks games.

MMA

MMA is a new sport in New York State. After a 20 year legal battle to have the sport legalized, the ban was finally lifted in 2016. In the past few years top UFC contenders have fought at Madison Square Garden. This list includes big name fighters such as Connor McGreggor, Eddie Alvarez, Georges St-Pierre, Michael Bisping, Cody Garbrandt, T.J. Dillashaw, Rose Namajunas and Joanna Jedrzejczyk.
