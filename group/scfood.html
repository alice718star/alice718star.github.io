Food in NYC

Street Food

No matter where you go in the Big Apple, you can always find amazing food on the go. Rushing? Just pick a famous New York style hot dog from a cart in the street. You can't agree on a cuisine with your friends? Then head to a Food hall or a street Food Market!

My favorites: Chelsea Market, Smorgasburg, Turnstyle, Canal street Market, Gotham Market, Industrial city, Eataly, Urbanspace, etc.

Restaurants

With more than 30,000 restaurants, it would take you more than 82 years to try every single one of them! Hopefully we have a good selection of apps for you that help you chosing.

Yelp, opentable, ubereats or course. But do you know "Just opened New York" to try the latest trends?

During "The restaurant weeks", in January and July, you can discover new tables at discounted prices.

New York Cuisine

One of the most iconic food from the Big Apple is maybe Bagels! A New York bagel has a shiny crust with a little bit of hardness to it and a nice glaze. The inside is very chewy, but not overly doughy. With cream cheese or eggs, it makes a perfect breakfast. Grab one from a "Pick a Bagel" store.

We can also mention Cheesecake, pizzas or just apples harvested in upstate New York!
